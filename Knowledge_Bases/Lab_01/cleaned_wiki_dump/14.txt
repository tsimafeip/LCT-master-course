Gomez began her acting career on children's television series Barney & Friends (2002–2004). In her teenage years, she rose to prominence for her lead role as Alex Russo in Disney Channel television series Wizards of Waverly Place (2007–2012).

Alongside her television career, Gomez appeared in films Another Cinderella Story (2008), Princess Protection Program (2009), Wizards of Waverly Place  The Movie (2009), Ramona and Beezus (2010), Monte Carlo (2011 film) Monte Carlo (2011), Spring Breakers (2012), Getaway (2013 film) Getaway (2013), The Fundamentals of Caring (2016), The Dead Don't Die (2019 film) The Dead Don't Die (2019), and A Rainy Day in New York (2019). She voiced character Mavis in Hotel Transylvania Hotel Transylvania film franchise (2012–2022). Gomez executive produced Netflix television series 13 Reasons Why (2017–2020) and Living Undocumented (2019) through her production company, July Moonhead Productions. She also executive produces and stars in HBO Max cooking series Selena + Chef (2020–present) and Hulu mystery comedy series Only Murders in Building (2021–present). The latter earned Gomez critical praise, and a nomination for Critics' Choice Television Award for Best Actress in a Comedy Series Best Actress in a Comedy series at 11th Critics' Choice Television Awards Critics' Choice Television Awards.
