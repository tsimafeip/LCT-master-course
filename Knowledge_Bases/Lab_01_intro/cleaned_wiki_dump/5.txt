'Shawn Peter Raul Mendes' (IPAc en ˈ m ɛ n d ɛ z; born August 8, 1998) is a Canadian singer and songwriter. He gained a following in 2013, posting song covers on video sharing application Vine (software) Vine. The following year, he caught attention of artist manager Andrew Gertler and Island Records Artists and repertoire A&R Ziggy Chareton, which led to him signing a deal with record label. Mendes's The Shawn Mendes EP self titled debut EP was released in 2014, followed by his debut studio album Handwritten (Shawn Mendes album) Handwritten in 2015. Handwritten debuted atop US Billboard 200 Billboard 200, making Mendes one of five artists ever to debut at number one before age of 18. The single "Stitches (Shawn Mendes song) Stitches" reached number one in UK and top 10 in US and Canada.

His second studio album Illuminate (Shawn Mendes album) Illuminate (2016) also debuted at number one in US, with its singles "Treat You Better (Shawn Mendes song) Treat You Better" and "There's Nothing Holdin' Me Back" reaching top 10 in several countries. His Shawn Mendes (album) self titled third studio album (2018) was supported by lead single "In My Blood (Shawn Mendes song) In My Blood". The album's number one debut in US made Mendes third youngest artist to achieve three number one albums. In 2019, he released hit singles "If I Can't Have You (Shawn Mendes song) If I Can't Have You" and "Señorita (Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello song) Señorita", with latter peaking atop US Billboard Hot 100 Billboard Hot 100. His fourth studio album, Wonder (Shawn Mendes album) Wonder (2020), made Mendes youngest male artist ever to top Billboard 200 with four studio albums. > web date 2020 12 13 title Shawn Mendes Achieves Fourth No. 1 Album on Billboard 200 Chart With 'Wonder' url 
Among List of awards and nominations received by Shawn Mendes his accolades, Mendes has won 13 Society of Composers, Authors and Music Publishers of Canada SOCAN awards, 10 MTV Europe Music Awards, eight Juno Awards, eight iHeartRadio MMVAs, two American Music Awards, and received three nominations for a Grammy Award and one nomination for a Brit Award. In 2018, Time (magazine) Time named Mendes as one of 100 most influential people in world on their Time 100 annual list.
