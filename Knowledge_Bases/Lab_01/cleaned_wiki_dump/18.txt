'Germany' (lang de Deutschland, IPA de ˈdɔʏtʃlant pron De Deutschland.ogg), officially 'Federal Republic of Germany',efn Lang de Bundesrepublik Deutschland, IPA de ˈbʊndəsʁepuˌbliːk ˈdɔʏtʃlant pron De Bundesrepublik Deutschland.ogg > book title Duden, Aussprachewörterbuch publisher Dudenverlag year 2005 isbn 978 3 411 04066 7 editor Mangold editor Max edition 6th pages 271, 53f language de > is a country in Central Europe. It is List of European countries by population second most populous country in Europe after Russia, and most populous member state of European Union. Germany is situated between Baltic Sea Baltic and North Sea North seas to north, and Alps to south; it covers an area of convert 357022 km2 sqmi, with a population of over 83 million within its 16 States of Germany constituent states. Germany borders Denmark to north, Poland and Czech Republic to east, Austria and Switzerland to south, and France, Luxembourg, Belgium, and Netherlands to west. The nation's capital and List of cities in Germany by population largest city is Berlin, and its financial centre is Frankfurt; largest urban area is Ruhr.

Various List of ancient Germanic peoples Germanic tribes have inhabited northern parts of modern Germany since classical antiquity. A region named Germania was documented before AD 100. In 10th century, German territories formed a central part of Holy Roman Empire. During 16th century, Northern Germany northern German regions became centre of Reformation Protestant Reformation. Following Napoleonic Wars and dissolution of Holy Roman Empire in 1806, German Confederation was formed in 1815. In 1871, Germany became a nation state when Unification of Germany most of German states unified into Kingdom of Prussia Prussian dominated German Empire. After World War I and German Revolution of 1918–1919, Empire was replaced by semi presidential Weimar Republic.

The Adolf Hitler's rise to power Nazi seizure of power in 1933 led to establishment of Nazi Germany a dictatorship, World War II, and Holocaust. After end of World War II in Europe and a period of Allied occupied Germany Allied occupation, Germany was divided into Federal Republic of Germany, generally known as West Germany, and German Democratic Republic, East Germany. The Federal Republic of Germany was a founding member of European Economic Community and European Union, while German Democratic Republic was a communist Eastern Bloc state and member of Warsaw Pact. After Peaceful Revolution fall of communism, German reunification saw New states of Germany former East German states join Federal Republic of Germany on German Unity Day 3 October 1990—becoming a federal parliamentary republic.

Germany is a great power with Economy of Germany a strong economy; it has List of sovereign states in Europe by GDP (nominal) largest economy in Europe, world's List of countries by GDP (nominal) fourth largest economy by nominal GDP, and List of countries by GDP (PPP) fifth largest by PPP. As a global leader in several industrial, Science and technology in Germany scientific and technological sectors, it is both world's List of countries by exports third largest exporter and List of countries by imports importer of goods. As a developed country, which List of countries by Human Development Index ranks very high on Human Development Index, it Social security in Germany offers social security and a Healthcare in Germany universal health care system, environmental protections, and a Higher education in Germany tuition free university education. Germany is a member of United Nations, NATO, G7, G20, and OECD. It has List of World Heritage sites in Germany third greatest number of UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
